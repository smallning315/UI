Apple's IOS 14.5 Update Might Surprise You

IOS 14 was announced at Apples WWDC in June 2020 and brought a whole cluster of new features.

Some of the leading features were widgets on the home screen, a new app library, a more convenient way to answer phone calls, picture and picture video, and the ability to pin conversations.

Many users took advantage of the update and redesigned their entire home screen with third party widget apps.

Many users evens showed their new layouts for a viral TikTok trend that displayed their different themes.
